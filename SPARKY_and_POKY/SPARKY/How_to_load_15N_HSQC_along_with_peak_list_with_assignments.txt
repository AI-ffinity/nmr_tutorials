Short Tutorial: how to load a 15N HSQC spectrum along with a peak lists containing assignments from BMRB for comparison.
Created by Lisa-Maria Weinhold, Feb 12, 2024


1. Open Sparky program
2. Open spectra (.ucsf format)
3. Command: vt 								#Adjust aspect (ppm) 
4. Command: ct 								#Adjust contour levels (Lowest)
5. Command: oz								#Adjust ornament sizes (Label size and peak size)
6. Command: rp								#Load peak list (.list format, columns layout: Assignment[AANoN-H] w1(N) w2(H))
7. Optional: Move label names manually
